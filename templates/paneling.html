Paneling

Wood paneling is an increasingly sought-after form of interior wall surface, being at a higher eye-line than flooring or molding, it is important to keep it looking nice. Whether your customer is looking for wide or narrow paneling, we can service your wood panel prefinishing needs. They may choose from a number of colored finishes, or leave it natural, but we can deal with all of it. Call 763-259-0061 for a free estimate.

Paneling is presanded, sealed on the backside with the front side receiving a durable UV cured urethane top coat with custom package requirements met.
